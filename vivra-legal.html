Vivra is a mobile application. Unlike websites, mobile apps do not use traditional browser cookies. However, we and our third-party partners use similar tracking technologies — including mobile advertising identifiers, local storage, and SDKs — to provide and improve our services.

Tracking Technologies We Use

Technology Type Purpose Can Opt Out?
Local Storage Essential Store your habits, settings, and preferences on your device No — required for app to function
Google AdMob SDK Advertising Serve personalised ads to free users Yes — opt out in device settings
Advertising ID (GAID) Advertising Identify your device for ad personalisation Yes — reset or opt out in device settings
Firebase Analytics Analytics Understand how users interact with the app Yes — disable in app settings

These technologies are necessary for Vivra to function properly. Without them, the app cannot save your data, remember your preferences, or maintain your login session.

If you use the free version of Vivra, Google AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising identifier to display personalised advertisements. This helps keep Vivra free for users who choose not to subscribe.
